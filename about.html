About EduDonate

We're on a mission to ensure every child has access to quality education and the tools they need to succeed in an increasingly digital world.

Our Mission

To empower underserved children and communities through personalized education sponsorship, community development, and innovative technology solutions that break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable pathways to success. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social change, and technology can amplify its impact when implemented thoughtfully and inclusively.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their geographic location or economic circumstances, has access to quality education and the digital tools necessary to thrive in the 21st century. We envision communities that are self-sustaining, digitally empowered, and equipped to continue their educational development long after our initial interventions.

Our Story

EduDonate was born from a simple observation: while technology was rapidly advancing education in developed countries, millions of children in underserved communities were being left behind. Our founders, a group of educators and technologists, saw an opportunity to bridge this gap. What started as a small initiative to provide tablets to a rural school in Southeast Asia has grown into a global movement. We've learned that sustainable impact requires more than just providing resourcesâ€”it requires building relationships, understanding local contexts, and empowering communities to drive their own progress. Today, EduDonate operates in 15 countries, supporting over 12,500 students and transforming entire communities. But our work is far from done. Every day, we're inspired by the resilience and potential of the children we serve, and we're committed to expanding our reach while deepening our impact.
